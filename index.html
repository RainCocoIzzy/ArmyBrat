What It's Like Growing Up As A Military Brat

If you look at me, I'm just a standard individual who experience all sort of life challenges while growing up. I'm an "armybrat" because I was born and raised as a dependent of the Philippine Army but there are also different brances of the Armed Forces who are also brats. While my dad was in a Presidential Security Group, he would take us to his office for a duty call and let us play around the Malacanang Palace building.
